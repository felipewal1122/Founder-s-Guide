How to Make Something Cool

1. Choose a Business Structure

Select the appropriate legal structure (e.g., SAS, S.A., Ltda).

2. Register the Company Name

Verify and register your company name with the Chamber of Commerce.

3. Draft the Bylaws (Estatutos)

Create the company bylaws, which include the purpose, partners, capital, and governance structure.

4. Register with the Chamber of Commerce

Submit your bylaws and required documents to obtain the Certificate of Incorporation.

5. Obtain the Tax ID (RUT)

Register with the DIAN (Tax Authority) to obtain the company’s tax ID (RUT).

6. Open a Corporate Bank Account

Use the incorporation documents and RUT to open a corporate bank account.

7. Register for Local Taxes

Register with your local municipality for ICA (industry and commerce tax).

8. Comply with Labor Regulations

Enroll employees in health, pension, and labor risk systems.
